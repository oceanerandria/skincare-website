Product Recommendations

Looking for more? Here are some great product recommendations that you can purchase in store:

Moisture Day Cream by The Body Shop. This specific product is toward all skin types although it is mostly used by people with dry skin. I personally have a mixed skin where my T-Zone is very greasy and the rest of my face is dry. This cream hydrated my skin, especially during winter. But it also helped get rid of the pimples and blackheads I had on my T-Zone area.

This Samphire Serum is based of sea-retinol, which is a type of vitamin A. Retinol cleanses your skin from any toxins. This particular product is also good for people who come in contact witht ht screen a lot since it protects the skin from blue lights and screens.

Pure Active Gel by Garnier is a great product for people who have greasy skin, especially if your skin is prone to acne and blackheads. It is a gel that you can wash you face with, and will make your skin brighter and softer. When used regularly, it will make your acne get better as its main purpose is to remove blackheads.

Lorenzo on managing an oily skin. I use four products. A face scrub from Nivea men to cleanse my face and to remove all toxins. Secondly, I use a foaming facial cleanser from CeraVe which cleanses and remove the sebum. I rinse off my face with water after those proudcts. Finally, I use a daily hydration lotion from Cetaphil which is to moisturize and hydrate my screen. I sometimes use sunscreen lotion from Life when it is sunny outside.

Mirana on maintaning a good skincare. I do not use much products when it comes to my face. The only product I use on a daily basis is the RevitaLift Night and Day cream from L'Oreal. This is to hydrate my face and also an anti-aging cream since I am in my 40s. From time to time, I use aloe vera masks or natural clay masks. We have these natural products in Madagascar so I try to make use of it.

Oceane on balancing a good skincare. I am a big fan of skincare, but I try to not overdo it. I still love keeping my skin in its natural state. Every two to three days, I use three different products. I first use the samphire serum to moisturize my skin but also to protect my skin from blue lights. I continue with the Vitamin E day cream by The Body Shop to hydrate my skin, especially with the cold.
